Privacy Policy for TTS Browser

Last updated: January 4, 2026

1. Introduction

TTS Browser ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application for Android devices.

Developer: jazekmon89
App Name: TTS Browser
Platform: Android (Google Play Store)

2. Information Collection and Use

TTS Browser itself does not directly collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require account creation, and we do not track your browsing history or the content you read with text-to-speech.

2.1 What We Don't Collect

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your browsing history or visited URLs
  • The text content you read with TTS
  • Your location data
  • Your personal files or photos
  • Login credentials or passwords

2.2 Third-Party Services

However, our app uses the following third-party service that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements

3. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob is a Google service that may collect and process certain information for the purpose of serving personalized ads.

3.1 Information AdMob May Collect

  • Device identifiers: Android Advertising ID (resettable)
  • IP address: Your approximate location based on IP
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, screen size
  • App usage data: App interactions and ad performance
  • Advertising data: Ad impressions, clicks, and interactions

3.2 How AdMob Uses This Information

  • To serve relevant advertisements to you
  • To measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • To prevent fraud and abuse
  • To improve ad quality and user experience

3.3 AdMob Privacy Policies

For more information about how Google uses data from apps that use Google services, please see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information
  • How Google uses data from sites or apps that use our services

4. Web Browsing

TTS Browser includes a web browser component that allows you to navigate to websites. Important:

  • We do not collect or store your browsing history
  • We do not access or store cookies from websites you visit
  • We do not monitor or log the URLs you enter
  • All web browsing is handled locally on your device

Please note that websites you visit may have their own privacy policies and may collect information according to their own terms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

5. Text-to-Speech Functionality

The text-to-speech (TTS) feature uses your device's built-in TTS engine provided by Android or third-party TTS engines you may have installed.

  • Text is processed locally on your device
  • We do not transmit or store the text content you read
  • TTS data is handled by your device's TTS engine according to its own privacy policy

6. Permissions Used

TTS Browser requires the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required to load webpages and display ads
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check internet connectivity

We do not request any sensitive permissions such as camera, microphone, location, or access to your files.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information.

The app is rated for users aged 13 and older in accordance with Google Play Store policies.

8. Data Security

Since TTS Browser does not collect or store personal data directly, there is minimal risk to your personal information through our app. All data processing happens locally on your device.

However, we cannot control the security practices of third-party services like AdMob. Please review their privacy policies for information about their security measures.
